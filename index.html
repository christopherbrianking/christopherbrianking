Christopher Brian King is a creative director, graphic designer, and illustrator based in New York. He has been featured seven times in the AIGA/Design Observer 50 Books/50 Covers exhibition, as well as in numerous publications including Print Magazine, which called his work “some of the most easily recognizable since Penguin’s orange spines.” In addition to his experience with both in-house and independent creative projects, he has taught at Parsons School of Design and spoken at the Type Directors Club, the School of Visual Arts, and the Dallas Society of Visual Communications.
